Tastes from home

Sneak peek of a few favorite recipes that are either traditional
or a fusion of the east and the west

Tastes can take you back in time, back to places we miss, back to people we love, and back to somewhere deep in our hearts. Hey, I'm Grace. I am a Taiwanese student studying in the US. I really enjoy baking, so sometimes I challenge myself to make desserts that can remind me of the small Island I come from.
