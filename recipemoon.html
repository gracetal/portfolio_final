Egg Yolk moon cake

I made my favorite kind of mooncake - egg yolk moon cake, this year. The mooncake is a small kind of pastry we eat as a dessert on the moon festival, which is usually in late September. The egg yolk moon cake has an egg yolk wrapped inside the bean paste filling, and is delicious. The recipe is a bit more complicated and time consuming. But for a taste of home, I’d say it’s worth it!

Ingredients:

• OIL PASTRY SKIN:
All purpose flour 67g
low-gluten flour 15g
powdered sugar 15g
Anhydrous cream 35g
water (room temperature) 37g

• PASTRY SKIN:
Low-gluten flour 115g
Anhydrous cream 52g

• FILLING:
10 salted egg yolk
red bean paste 225g
rum 10g

• DECORATE:
yolk 34g (2 pieces)
water 3.4g
raw black sesame 6g

Steps:

FILLING:
1. Soak salted egg yold in rum
2. Seperate bean paste into 10 portion
3. Stuff the egg yolk in a ball of bean paste, set aside

Oil Pastry Skin:
1. Mix dry ingredients and slowly add water
2. add cream
3. Roll into 10 balls, set aside to rest for 15 min

Short Pastry mix:
1. Mix dry ingredients and add cream
2.Roll into 10 balls, set aside to rest for 15 min

Assemble:
1. Stuff the Short Pastry mix in the ball of oil pastry skin
2. Flatten the dough, and roll it up. Let it rest for 10 min.
3. Flatten the dough, and roll it up in a different direction.
4. Roll flat the dough, and stuff the filling in the dough.
5. Mix the egg yolk and water, and brush on top of the pastry
6. Sprinkle black sesame for decoration

Oven:
1. Oven should be preheated o 356°F (180°C).
2. Bake for 30 minutes.
